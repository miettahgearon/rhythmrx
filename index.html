Rhythm Rx

To assist physical therapists and patients in identifying preferred songs that align with the advised tempo for a given intervention, this music finder (RhythmRx) provides users with a simple selection of songs listed by BPM.

By inputting a preferred musical artist and intended tempo range (by BPM value/tempo name), Rhythm Rx can search its database to find songs recorded by that preferred artist that are therapeutically effective.
